This is a small game I made back in high school using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Source files can be found here. Depending on your screen size you may need to zoom out in the browser so the text log will fit next to the map.

You must save your life and continent by fighting off the invaders. Surely the unmotivated infantry will give up and go home if you manage to defeat their king. You must defeat one enemy per block to challenge an enemy in the next block. Keep fighting until you can challenge the king.

To fight, first select a weapon to use and then an enemy to use it on. Each weapon deals a specific amount of damage and has a certain percent accuracy. These values are equal to the enemies equivalent weapons. You can use a bomb to deal 8 damage to an enemy without fail, or use an elixir to raise your HP by 30. Good Luck!
